# Support

Linux Made Sane Community Edition is free for personal, homelab, educational, non-profit, and internal business use. Commercial organisations may use LMS Community Edition internally to manage their own infrastructure without buying a license.

Paid support and consulting are available from Richard D. Kiernan. Buying support does not change your right to use Community Edition internally; it buys help, priority, and commercial engagement.

Paid options may include:

- onboarding and installation help
- production hardening
- AI provider and local AI configuration
- Cloudflare, Caddy, and Tailscale setup
- enterprise integrations
- priority fixes
- managed Linux Made Sane services

Commercial licensing is separate from support. See [COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md](./COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md) for when a commercial agreement is required.
